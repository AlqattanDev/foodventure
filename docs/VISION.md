# VISION — Foodventure: Khaliji Edition

The full ambition, preserved. **None of this is built in V1.** V1 is one stall, one dish (Halwa Bahrainiya), three variations — see `../STATUS.md` and `../BUILD_PROMPT.md`. These layers come only after the core cook→sell→earn→unlock loop is proven fun on a phone.

## Core fantasy (full)
A young Gulf chef starts with a tiny stall and builds a thriving traditional Khaliji food empire. Hands-on cooking mastery first, empire second: you get genuinely good at real recipes before you scale.

## Roadmap layers (post-V1)
- **More dishes & stations:** Luqaimat, Regag bread, Oman Halwa, and beyond — each its own station and cooking mini-game.
- **Supply chain:** order real ingredients online (dates, premium saffron, rose water), quality tiers that affect final dish stars, inventory + expiration to avoid waste.
- **Staff & hiring:** hire chefs/helpers; staff can only work dishes they've personally mastered (same mini-games as training); Gulf nationalities with specialties.
- **Restaurant expansion:** more tables/chairs, traditional majlis seating, bigger kitchens, equipment upgrades (precise stoves, copper pots, traditional ovens).
- **Multiple cities:** Bahrain → Oman → UAE → Saudi → Kuwait, each with local customer preferences and signature dishes.
- **Reputation & customers:** locals, tourists, food bloggers, royal family; high-star dishes earn big tips and glowing reviews.
- **Events:** Ramadan specials, Eid sweets, Gulf wedding feasts, Gargee'an nights — limited-time high-reward dishes.
- **Family legacy:** unlock old family recipes as you progress.
- **Spice trading:** rare spices from virtual souqs granting cooking bonuses.
- **Weather/time system:** hot afternoons quieter; cool evenings and Ramadan nights packed.

## Guardrails (carry into every layer)
Educational + authentic (real recipes, real ingredients — nothing invented), respectful to Khaliji culture, satisfying progression, never grindy, never pay-to-win. Mastery gates money gates expansion — skill matters more than spending.

## Why staged
The whole bet is "is the cooking fun and does it look stunning?" One dish answers that in weeks. Everything above is where the empire grows once the answer is yes.
